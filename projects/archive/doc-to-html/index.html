My favorite place - Krankies

I’m not much of a go out to eat person but this place is the main place I take people to go when they visit me lol. They have a small menu but I think their biscuits are pretty good - my favorite being their Krankies Klasic (fried chicken with hot honey in a biscuit). They also have pretty good coffee and tea - I got a sugar plum chai latte the other day that was quite festive. They are located right in the center of downtown Winston Salem in what's called Innovation Quarter so it's easy to get something there and then just walk around afterwards.
